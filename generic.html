Bandung, 3 - 4 May 2025

2nd Place
Datavidia Arkavidia 9.0

In a stacked finals filled with incredibly talented individuals from across Indonesia, our team (me, Rifqi and Favian) proudly secured 2nd place at Datavidia – ARKAVIDIA 9.0, a national data science competition hosted by Himpunan Mahasiswa Informatika (HMIF) ITB. During the preliminary round, We were tasked with forecasting national commodity prices. Instead of using typical GBDT or statistical models, we explored efficient deep learning-based forecasting methods. The final round involved a 5-hour live coding session, followed by a presentation the next day. The task: predict property rental prices. We focused on engineering text features using TF-IDF and optimized a LightGBM model. Our model achieved the best accuracy, though just barely. While we fell short during the presentation, I’m proud of our performance, especially while juggling our undergraduate theses.

Surabaya, 20 October 2024

3rd Winner
Data Mining ACTION 2024 (Academic Competition of Data Science)

On October 20th, 2024, Rifqi Hafizuddin, Favian Sulthan Wafi and I competed in the final round of the Data Mining - Academic Competition of Data Science (Action) 2024, hosted by Universitas Negeri Surabaya, and are thrilled to have secured 3rd place. For the preliminary round, we submitted a paper titled “Aplikasi Fractional Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks dalam Pemantauan Deforestasi di IKN,” where we applied data science to tackle environmental sustainability issues. In the final round, we faced a one-day Kaggle case study on air quality detection, followed by presenting our solution the following day.

Jakarta, 4 - 5 October 2024

Finalist
8th Open Innovation 2024

I participated in the Health Research & Innovation Competition organized by IMERI (Indonesia Medical Education and Research Institute) FK UI (Faculty of Medicine, University of Indonesia). The competition was themed "Sustainable Future: Convergence of Medical Technology and Green Solutions for a Better Life." Collaborating with a diverse team from backgrounds in health, business, and technology, we developed a prototype of a skin cancer detection app capable of identifying over 13 cancer types and other skin conditions. Unfortunately, we only managed to get into the final stage (Top 20 Finalist).

Jakarta, 22 September 2024

5th Place Winner
Statistics Olympiad Poisson Statistics Competition 2024

On September 22, 2024, Rifqi Hafizuddin and I competed in the final round of the Statistics Olympiad - Poisson Statistics Competition, organized by Politeknik Statistika STIS, where we secured 5th place. During the final round, we presented our paper, titled “Pemanfaatan Official Statistics dalam Pemodelan Angka Harapan Hidup dengan Regresi Spasial,” focused on leveraging official statistics to maximize the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by identifying key factors affecting life expectancy.

Bandung, 29 July - 2 August 2024

Most Scalable Algorithm
Big Data Challenge Satria Data 2024

The Big Data Challenge (BDC) is a competition aimed at providing solutions to real-world problems through analytical, mathematical, and statistical approaches. The competition consists of three stages: (1) preliminary round, (2) semifinal, and (3) final.

From 29th July to 1st August 2024, I had the privilege of attending the Satria Data 2024 Final Round at Telkom University. My team and I competed in the Big Data Challenge division against 424 teams from all across Indonesia. This competition was held by Pusat Prestasi Nasional, under Kementerian Pendidikan dan Kebudayaan (Ministry of Education and Culture Republic of Indonesia). Alongside my incredible teammates, Rifqi Hafizuddin and Adawia Ananda, we presented our paper titled “Election 2024 Tweet Analysis: Sentiment Analysis, Topic Modeling, and Social Network Analysis Approaches in Understanding Perceptions and Dynamics of the Presidential Election”. Our team was honored to receive the title of The Most Scalable Algorithm for classifying topics of tweets regarding the 2024 Presidential Election.
